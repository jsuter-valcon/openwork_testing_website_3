What if you need a little support

When it comes to stepping onto the housing ladder, it's not uncommon to seek a little assistance. Fortunately, the UK government offers a range of options designed to support individuals in purchasing their dream home. At Strong Mortgage Solutions, we specialize in guiding you through these government schemes and finding the right mortgage to suit your needs. With our extensive experience in helping people embark on the property ladder using these schemes, you can trust us to provide expert advice and assistance every step of the way.


Some of the government schemes we can advise you on include:

  • Shared Ownership: This scheme enables you to purchase a share of a property while paying rent on the remaining portion, making homeownership more affordable and accessible.
  • Deposit Unlock: With this scheme, you can unlock your deposit by borrowing a portion of it, allowing you to reach the required deposit amount for your property purchase.
  • Right to Buy: This scheme grants eligible council or housing association tenants the right to purchase their rented property at a discounted price, providing a pathway to homeownership.

Throughout the process, we are here to address any questions or concerns you may have. Our dedicated team is committed to helping you make informed decisions and securing the best possible outcome. Contact us today to explore the government schemes available and begin your journey towards owning your ideal home.

Investment Property

Property investment has gained significant popularity as a means to generate income, whether for the present or retirement. In today's landscape, various property investment types cater to different investment strategies. At Strong Mortgage Solutions, we offer expertise in guiding you through the options best suited to your preferences:

  • Buy to Let: This investment strategy involves purchasing a property with the intention of renting it out, allowing you to benefit from rental income and potential capital appreciation.
  • Let to Buy: With this approach, you can convert your current residential property into a rental property while simultaneously purchasing a new home.
  • HMO (House in Multiple Occupation): HMOs involve renting out individual rooms within a property to multiple tenants, often yielding higher rental returns.
  • Holiday Let: Investing in a property for holiday rentals can offer a profitable income stream, particularly in popular tourist destinations.
  • Experienced Landlord: If you have prior experience in property investment, we can provide tailored solutions to expand your portfolio and maximise returns.
  • Limited Company Buy to Let: Investing through a limited company can have tax advantages and provide a structured approach to property investment.

Our comprehensive understanding of property investment enables us to offer tailored advice and solutions specific to your investment goals. Whether you're a seasoned investor or just starting out, we're here to assist you at every step. Contact us today to explore the diverse property investment options available and optimise your investment strategy.

Bespoke mortgages

In certain property transactions, a specialised mortgage may be required to meet specific needs and circumstances. At Strong Mortgage Solutions, we understand the importance of tailored solutions, and we offer expert assistance in various areas:


  • Commercial Lending: If you're seeking a mortgage for a commercial property to support your business endeavors, we can guide you through the process and help secure the financing you need.
  • Bridging Loans: When time is of the essence, such as in auction purchases or quick completions, bridging loans provide short-term financing solutions to bridge the gap until permanent financing is arranged.
  • Second Charges: If you require additional funds but don't want to disturb your existing mortgage, a second charge mortgage can be an option, allowing you to leverage the equity in your property.
  • Adverse Credit: If you have experienced credit challenges in the past, we specialize in assisting clients with adverse credit histories, providing access to mortgage options tailored to your circumstances.
  • Equity Release: For homeowners aged 55 and over, equity release allows you to unlock the value of your property and access funds for various purposes, such as supplementing retirement income or undertaking home improvements.

Our team of mortgage experts is well-versed in these specialised areas and can provide personalised advice and solutions to meet your specific requirements. Contact us today to discuss your needs and let us help you navigate the complexities of obtaining a specialist mortgage.
